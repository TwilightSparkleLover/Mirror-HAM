Under the bar that reads tUME v00.2620q is a menu bar that appears 
when you click on it with the left mouse button. 

Select Project|Load. Load any file with the .MAP extension. 

You can switch between the two panes by pressing SPACE. 

You can scroll through the windows within a pane by using '1' and '2'. 

For additional keyboard commands, find the [KEYS] section of tUME.ini.
